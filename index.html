ℹ HOW TO PLAY

VORTEX PULSE: SYNTH-RUNNER
── CONTROLS ──
MOUSE / TOUCH — move your ship
WASD / ARROW KEYS — keyboard movement
SPACE / RIGHT-CLICK / DOUBLE-TAP — throw grenade
ESC — pause game
Ship auto-fires — aim by moving towards enemies
── COMBAT ──
Kill enemies → earn SCRAP ⬡ — spend in the shop
Pick up glowing drops for shields, grenades & temp guns
Shield absorbs hits first — regenerates slowly over time
Lose all lives → game over. Lives don't regen mid-run
── ENEMIES ──
Drone — basic. Tank — slow, very tough.
Speeder — fast, fragile. Sniper — precision shots.
Splitter — fires 3-way spread. Phantom — swerves.
Heavy — giant, armoured, fires double shots.
── BOSSES ──
A boss spawns every 5 levels — they cycle in order:
Overlord — fan shots & aimed spirals
The Void — teleports, vortex & ring patterns
Hammer — barrage sweeps across the screen
Nexus — ring & mirror bullet patterns
Dreadnought — all patterns, most hp
── WEAPONS ──
13 budget guns — 100 to 480 ⬡. Always firing, no reload.
10 standard guns — 300 to 800 ⬡. More powerful, specialty roles.
10 legendary guns — 20k to 300k ⬡. 3s fire / 4s reload.
8 god tier guns — 1M to 10M ⬡. 3s fire / 4s reload. Absolute destruction.
Buy in shop → tap EQUIP to switch guns anytime
Tap PAUSE FIRE button to stop shooting (useful for stealth)
── ACCOUNTS & SAVES ──
Create an account to save progress across sessions
Up to 5 save slots — auto-saves on boss kill & game over
Export Code — share your account to any browser or device
Account menu → 📋 EXPORT CODE → paste on new device to transfer
Leaderboard tracks your highest level reached
── SHIP HANGAR ──
Customise hull shape, colour, wings, cockpit, engine & trail
All parts purchased with SCRAP ⬡ — cosmetic only
